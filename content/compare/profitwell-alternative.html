Frequently Asked Questions

My ProfitWell by Paddle numbers look completely different from Stripe. Which is right?

Data discrepancies between ProfitWell and Stripe are common and usually come down to how each tool defines MRR — how they handle one-time charges, currency conversion, trial periods, and subscription status. Baremetrics' MRR calculation is configurable, and the team will get on a call to find the specific discrepancy and walk through exactly what's happening. Stripe is typically the source of truth — Baremetrics is built to match and explain it.

How does Baremetrics Recover compare to ProfitWell Retain?

Both help you recover revenue lost to failed payments. The key differences: Recover emails are fully customizable — your copy, sender name, domain, and branding. Retain emails cannot be edited (confirmed by G2 reviews). Recover is $129/month flat with no commission and a money-back guarantee; Retain is performance-based with a custom quote and no published rate. Recover sends from your company. Retain sends from Paddle.

What's the difference between ProfitWell's forecasting and Forecast+?

ProfitWell offers basic end-of-month projections from your payment processor. Baremetrics' Forecast+ solution connects to QuickBooks or Xero, ingests your actual P&L, and produces a full financial dashboard: runway, burn rate, expense breakdown by department, CAC, and budget-vs-actual tracking. One is a subscription projection. The other is a CFO dashboard. Included in every Baremetrics plan.

Does Baremetrics have a free plan like ProfitWell?

No. The lowest entry point is $49/month on the Accelerator plan, available to SaaS companies under $30K MRR. For founders who only need core metrics and aren't ready to pay, ProfitWell Metrics is a legitimate starting point. Baremetrics is the right move when you need to go deeper — segmentation, financial forecasting, or branded revenue recovery.

Does Baremetrics have a revenue recognition solution?

Not currently. If revenue recognition is critically important to your business, get in touch — we're happy to talk through your needs and see if Baremetrics is still a fit.

Why do companies switch from ProfitWell to Baremetrics?

The most common reasons: they need deeper segmentation beyond ProfitWell's basic plan filters; they want financial forecasting connected to their actual P&L; they're using Retain but want recovery emails that come from their company rather than Paddle; they want flat, predictable pricing instead of a performance-based model; and they want a support team that gets on calls and builds things with them.

Can I switch from ProfitWell easily?

Yes. CSV import handles historical data, and the team will walk through migration on a call. Most customers have live data within a day.
