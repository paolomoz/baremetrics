What is Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)?

Customer Lifetime Value (LTV) is a crucial SaaS metric that predicts the total revenue you can expect from a customer throughout their entire relationship with your business. It helps you make informed decisions about customer acquisition costs, marketing spend, and business growth strategies.

The basic LTV formula is: LTV = ARPU ÷ Churn Rate, where ARPU is your Average Revenue Per User and Churn Rate is the percentage of revenue lost each month.

Why LTV Matters for SaaS:

  • Determine sustainable customer acquisition costs (CAC)
  • Optimize pricing and retention strategies
  • Forecast revenue and business growth
  • Make data-driven product and marketing decisions
  • Evaluate the health of your business model
