Some of the most vital business decisions depend on real-time predictive analytics solutions, advanced technologies ecosystem (machine learning algorithms, big data, business intelligence, etc.), and market segmentation.

Baremetrics

This SaaS analytics tool analyzes, churns optimization, and organizes large chunks of data. It allows forecasting and segmentation of data such as MRR, sign-up data, lifetime subscriptions, etc.

The Benchmark feature of Baremetrics allows you to compare your business growth with other companies in the industry.

It also helps to cut down hours of data handling workflow time and is loaded with user-friendly features

Google Analytics

Google Analytics is among the oldest and commonly used customer data platforms by web analysts.

It generates details and statistics about visitors to your website along with information on the average time a visitor stays on a page, source from where they’re coming, and how often they come.

However, the tool has some drawbacks in terms of customer care and support.

Optimizely

Optimizely is a new service but offers wide-ranging, powerful results that help evaluate and optimize your website through A/B testings.

It allows you to gain extraordinary experiences, make impactful marketing decisions, and innovate at a faster pace. However, many users have complained about the difficulty of integrating it with other digital analytics tools.

Kissmetrics

Kissmetrics is one of the easiest tools which provides customer insights and engages them through highly customized Facebook and email marketing campaigns.

It tracks the visitor’s journey on your website and provides you with details about the features that are most useful for them. Yet, some users reported concerns with data exports, visual aesthetics, and navigation.

Sprout Social

Sprout Social is a tool for businesses who wish to understand the behavior of their social media audience.

It allows you to monitor and engage with messages across social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter by setting up chatbots.

Baremetrics is also diving deep into how social media software companies retain customers. Yet, if you are looking to connect to different social networks, Sprout Social might not be your best option due to its limitations in social media connections.

Mixpanel

Mixpanel is a self-serving tool for consumer insights trusted by more than 26,000 businesses worldwide. It helps you understand how your consumers communicate with your services and other data related to them.

It is one of the best analytical methods out there as it allows easy integrations and flexible APIs. Yet, some users reported that Mixpanel gets slow with the increase of data volume.
