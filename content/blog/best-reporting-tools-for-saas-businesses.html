Zoho Analytics

Zoho Analytics allows easy collaboration with the team in real-time to analyze and share reports collectively, export reports for online access, and schedule them as mails.

You can leverage the pre-built visualizations using the drag-and-drop API interface. Zoho Analytics focuses on easy usability and automation.

Zoho Analytics pricing: The cost structure ranges between $25-$495 per month according to the number of users and data.

Sisense

Sisense is a diverse end-to-end reporting solution that offers user-friendly, on-premise installation.

If your company manages a massively evolving data source, then Sisense may be a perfect choice for you as it provides ad hoc reports in real-time.

Sisense pricing: Available on request.

Holistics

Holistics is a business intelligence tool with SQL which generates ad hoc queries and delivers data to everyone. Data analysts and non-technical users can take advantage of the strong analytics features of Holistics.

Holistics pricing: Paid plans range between $50-$500 per month.

Sprout Social

Sprout Social is an extensive tool for managing social media. It allows you to carry out a qualitative analysis of topics, hashtags, and keywords through your cross-channel social features.

Sprout Social pricing: The paid plans start from $99 per month.

InfoSet

InfoSet Query is an easy-to-use and intuitive general SAP reporting tool. You can access all kinds of data sources stored anywhere in the Personal Resources System by directly selecting fields and output areas.

To build reports using InfoSet Query, you don’t need programming skills. Alongside these tools, there are some key business intelligence platforms used by marketers and a wide range of industry experts.

These tools include PowerBI, Qlik, Tableau.
