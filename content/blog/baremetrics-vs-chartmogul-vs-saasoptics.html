Baremetrics vs. ChartMogul vs. SaaSOptics

By Lea LeBlanc on March 14, 2023 Last updated on April 28, 2026

style
article-body

Subscription reporting is the key to growing a SaaS business, but how do you know which analytics platform is right for you? Baremetrics, ChartMogul, and SaaSOptics are three popular solutions to consider.

Read on to learn more about the differences between these platforms in terms of capabilities, ease of use, UI, customer support, and price. This will help you decide which tool fits the unique needs of your SaaS or subscription business.

About Baremetrics

Baremetrics is a metrics, engagement, and dunning platform built from the ground up for SaaS and subscription companies. The platform’s Smart Dashboards provide a simple and intuitive view of your metrics to drive smarter business decisions.

Besides tracking SaaS metrics, Baremetrics also provides automated capabilities for improving revenue recovery and reducing churn. By giving you the dunning and customer engagement tools you need to take action, you can adopt a data-driven approach to scale your SaaS business.

About ChartMogul

ChartMogul is a SaaS analytics tool for measuring, understanding, and growing subscription revenue. The platform is primarily focused on providing accurate metrics that can be viewed using data analysis tools and charts.

While ChartMogul has a lot of great visualization features, it doesn’t have as many features for taking action on the data when compared to a platform like Baremetrics. That means SaaS companies will likely need to pair ChartMogul with another platform for automated dunning and revenue recovery.

About SaaSOptics

SaaSOptics is a platform for subscription management, revenue recognition, expense tracking, and SaaS metrics. The platform focuses on B2B SaaS companies that require a wide range of financial reporting capabilities.

However, with so many options comes complexity. In contrast, Baremetrics focuses on the most essential metrics and offers automated features. The result is a much simpler learning curve that reduces the time required to start generating insights.

Compared to ChartMogul and SaaSOptics, Baremetrics offers the most comprehensive set of features essential to growing a subscription business, all in one tool.

Which Is Right for You: Baremetrics, ChartMogul, or SaaSOptics?

While all three platforms are built for SaaS and subscription businesses, each one has different strengths and weaknesses depending on what you’re looking for.

ChartMogul offers data analytics and segmentation, but it doesn’t provide many features for taking action on these insights. SaaSOptics has comprehensive features for B2B SaaS companies and might be a bit complicated for small to mid-sized companies.

Baremetrics is an easy-to-use analytics platform that tracks all of the most essential metrics for SaaS and subscription businesses. The platform also goes beyond subscription analytics by providing automated tools for dunning and churn insights, as well as advanced financial forecasting and scenario planning.

FAQ

  • What is the difference between Baremetrics and ChartMogul for subscription analytics? Baremetrics is an all-in-one subscription analytics and revenue growth platform, while ChartMogul focuses primarily on data visualization and subscription metrics reporting. The core difference is what each tool lets you do with your data once you have it. ChartMogul provides strong charting and segmentation capabilities, but it does not offer automated dunning or failed payment recovery, meaning SaaS teams need a separate tool to act on revenue at risk. Baremetrics combines real-time MRR tracking, churn analytics, advanced financial forecasting, and a built-in dunning feature called Recover in a single platform, so subscription businesses can move from insight to action without stitching together multiple tools.
  • What platforms offer automated failed payment recovery for subscription businesses? Baremetrics offers automated failed payment recovery through its Recover feature, which is built directly into the subscription analytics platform rather than sold as a separate tool. Recover automatically retries failed charges and sends customer-facing email sequences to recover revenue before it is permanently lost to involuntary churn. Because Recover sits inside the same platform as your MRR and churn dashboards, you can see the direct impact of recovered payments on your monthly recurring revenue in real time. For SaaS businesses on Stripe, Braintree, or Recurly, this means involuntary churn is tracked, acted on, and measured in one place, without any additional integration work.
  • How do I choose between Baremetrics and ChartMogul based on my SaaS company's needs? Choosing between Baremetrics and ChartMogul depends on whether you need subscription analytics alone or a broader toolkit that includes revenue recovery and churn insights alongside your metrics. ChartMogul is a reasonable fit if your primary need is data visualization and segmentation, and you are comfortable using separate tools for dunning and forecasting. Baremetrics is the stronger choice if you want MRR tracking, churn analytics, cancellation insights, advanced financial forecasting, and automated failed payment recovery in a single platform without setup complexity. For SaaS founders and finance leads managing $10K to $10M MRR who need to act on their subscription data, not just view it, Baremetrics removes the need to assemble a stack of point solutions.
  • How can I measure and reduce involuntary churn caused by failed payments in my subscription business? The most effective way to measure and reduce involuntary churn is to first separate it from voluntary cancellations inside your subscription analytics platform, so you know exactly how much MRR is at risk from payment failures rather than deliberate cancellations. Once you have that visibility, you can activate automated dunning to retry failed charges and trigger recovery email sequences before the subscription lapses entirely. From there, tracking recovered MRR over time gives you a clear picture of how much revenue your dunning process is actually saving each month. Baremetrics handles all of this natively: the Recover feature retries failed payments automatically and the churn dashboard keeps voluntary and involuntary churn separated, so you are always working from accurate numbers.
  • How do I benchmark my SaaS churn rate against similar subscription companies? Benchmarking your churn rate against comparable SaaS companies requires access to aggregated, real-world data from businesses at a similar MRR range, growth stage, and business model, rather than relying on industry averages pulled from blog posts. Baremetrics publishes open benchmark data drawn from hundreds of SaaS companies, covering metrics like monthly churn rate, LTV, ARPU, and MRR growth, giving subscription founders and finance teams a credible reference point for evaluating their own performance. Pairing those benchmarks with your own cohort-level churn data, broken down by pricing tier, billing interval, or customer segment, gives you the full picture needed to identify whether a churn problem is structural or isolated to a specific part of your subscriber base.
