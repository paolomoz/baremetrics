New Metric: Refunds

By Josh Pigford on March 19, 2014 Last updated on April 23, 2026

style
article-body

This week is shaping up to be quite busy with new features!

On Monday we launched Notifications, which let you stay in the loop on important business transactions. Wednesday we made a new Revenue Churn metric available so you can track not only user churn but also how much revenue you’re churning.

And now today we’ve got yet another new metric for you: Refunds!

Using the new Refunds metric you can see all of your Stripe refunds in one place and see overall trends in addition to who you refunded in any given timeframe.

Josh Pigford
